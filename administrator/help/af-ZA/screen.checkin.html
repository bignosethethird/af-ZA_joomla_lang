Globale Check-in

-- Voorlopige Weergawe

Globale Inteken:

Wanneer 'n Gebruiker redigeers 'n lêer, Joomla changes sy status to, "Checked Out". In effect, die lêer is locked en only die Gebruiker wie has "checked it out" has permission to work metit. Hierdie is 'n security feature that prevents two Gebruikers van redigeering 'n document at dieselfde time, thus preventing loss of data upon saving.

'n Inhoud Item remains 'Checked Out' until die Gebruiker clicks 'Stoor' of 'Kanselleer' while redigeering die Inhoud Item, at which time die Inhoud Item is "Checked In". However, if there's 'n connection error, die gebruiker presses die 'Back' ikoon in his/her browser, of die Gebruiker chooses ander menu item sonder saving of canceling die work in progress, die Inhoud Item remains 'Checked Out'. Hierdie means no one kan redigeer it. Hierdie is usually reported of experienced as Gebruikers being unable to redigeer of access items, en kan be verified by looking at die kolom labeled "Checked Out" in die Seksie, Kategorie, of Item lêer lys .

Joomla has 'n protocol, called "Globale Checkin" that allows 'n administrateur to unlock all Inhoud Items that is currently "Checked Out".

To perform die "Globale Check In", select Stelsel > Globale Check In van die Administrateur Menubar. After performing die "Globale Check In", Joomla keer terug na 'n lys showing how many items, en van which tables, were Checked back in.

Waarskuwing: Be very careful in doing hierdie as anyone wie is redigeering 'n document at that very moment kan het his of her work lost.

Stelsel Info
Die Stelsel Info bladsy provides detailed inligting about die system that jou Joomla site is deployed on. Hierdie includes weergawe inligting vir die site OS, My SQL, PHP, Web server, en Joomla. It ook includes die relevante PHP settings.

Die inligting on hierdie bladsy is very useful to Joomla developers when they is trying to help jy fix 'n bug metJoomla. Om hierdie rede it dit 'n goeie praktyk om hierdie inligting saam met jou foutrapporte of ondersteunings-versoeke na die Joomla forums te pos.

Die stelsel inligting kan be reached by selecting 'Hulp' van die Administrasie Menu, en dan 'Stelsel Informasie' te kies.
